01
Numbers as Encoded Frequencies

In ordinary construction, numbers are labels — you use 12 screws because that's how many fit, or 30cm because that's a convenient length. In sacred device work, numbers are not labels. They are vibrational patterns physically encoded into matter.

When you wind a coil to 108 turns, you are not just wrapping wire 108 times. You are encoding the digit-sum 9 — completion, self-return — into the electromagnetic geometry of the coil. When you cut wire to a cubit length, you are tuning that ring to resonate at a specific measurable frequency. The number and the physics are, in this context, the same thing.
