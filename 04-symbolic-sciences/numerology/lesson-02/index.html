02
The Pythagorean System

All name-based calculations use the Pythagorean system — the alphabet assigned values 1–9 in a repeating cycle. Commit this table to memory. You will use it for every name calculation in this module.

123456789
A J SB K TC L UD M VE N WF O XG P YH Q ZI R

A=1, B=2 ... I=9, then J=1, K=2 ... R=9, then S=1 ... Z=8. Master Numbers 11, 22, 33 are never reduced in your chart. Y is a vowel when it carries the vowel sound in a syllable (e.g. Lynn, Mary).

03
The Life Path Number

Your most important number. Derived from your full birth date — each part reduced separately, then combined. Describes the road you were born to travel.

1
Write your birth date as Month / Day / Year
2
Reduce each part separately to a single digit (preserve 11, 22, 33)
3
Add the three results together, then reduce once more

WORKED EXAMPLE · November 28, 1984

PartCalculationResult
Month: November = 111+12
Day: 282+8=10 → 1+01
Year: 19841+9+8+4=22 → 2+24
Total2+1+4=77

Life Path 7 = The Seeker. A life of wisdom, introspection, and spiritual truth.

04
Expression, Soul Urge & Personality

All three are derived from your full birth name exactly as it appears on your birth certificate. Always use your birth name — not married, preferred, or nickname.

EXPRESSION

Assign a number to every letter. Sum each name separately, reduce, then add the name totals and reduce again.

SOUL URGE

Use only the vowels (A, E, I, O, U). Sum all vowel values across the full name, then reduce.

PERSONALITY

Use only the consonants. Sum all consonant values across the full name, then reduce.

The gap between your Soul Urge and Personality numbers reveals the distance between your inner world and your outer presentation.
That gap is itself a teaching.

This Week: Your Blueprint
Practical Application · Do This

Step 1 — Calculate all four numbers using the calculator above. Write them down by hand. Don't rush. Allow each number to land before moving to the next.

Step 2 — Sit with your Life Path first. Read its meaning slowly. Notice what resonates — not what you wish were true, but what you already recognise as true.

Step 3 — Look for the gap. Compare your Soul Urge to your Personality number. Where are they aligned? Where do they differ? This gap often reveals your most important integration work.

Step 4 — Tell someone your Life Path number and invite them to share theirs. Notice how naturally the conversation deepens. Numbers are a shared language for self-disclosure.
